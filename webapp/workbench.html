Trading Decision Stack

John validates the company. Max validates the trade.

John Institutional Analyst

John decides whether the company deserves capital. His answer is based on fundamentals, valuation, revisions, catalysts, peer quality, and downside risk.

Financial statements FMP fundamentals SEC 10-K / 10-Q / 8-K Analyst estimates Peer multiples ROIC / margins / FCF Balance sheet risk News and catalysts Macro regime Ownership / sentiment
John output Buy / Watch / Avoid
Max Technical Analyst

Max decides whether price gives us an executable trade. His answer is based on trend, structure, liquidity, Fibonacci, Elliott Wave, ICT, and reward/risk.

Daily / weekly OHLCV 20 / 50 / 200D averages RSI / MACD / ATR Relative strength Swing highs / lows Fibonacci levels Elliott Wave state Liquidity sweeps Fair value gaps Order blocks
Max output Enter / Wait / Avoid
Final Output Trading Decision

The final call combines John and Max into Buy, Wait, Watch, or Avoid with conviction, entry zone, stop, targets, reward/risk, and data confidence.
