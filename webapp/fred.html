Workflow

How Fred turns a market read into an options ticket

01 Regime split

Classify the ticker as growth, value, event-driven, or volatility dislocation.

02 Chain scan

Filter by liquidity, spread, IV rank, skew, term structure, open interest, and gamma concentration.

03 Strike and maturity

Choose delta, expiry, and structure against expected move, breakeven, and catalyst timing.

04 Ticket review

Log thesis, max loss, target, stop, time stop, Greek exposures, and earnings risk.

05 Exit discipline

Close when target, invalidation, time decay, IV crush, or catalyst completion says the edge is gone.
